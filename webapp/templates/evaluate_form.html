Warning: Don't Use Raw Google Translate!

Using Google Translate directly will give artificially LOW scores! This is because BLEU (translation quality metric) measures exact word and phrase matching.

Why? Even if both your system and Google Translate are semantically correct, different word choices (e.g., "Hi" vs "Hello") cause low BLEU scores.

Tips for Ground Truth:
  • Transcript: Should match exactly what was said in the video (word-for-word)
  • Translation: Should be a high-quality reference translation
  • Best Practice: Start with your system's output, then verify and correct errors
  • Avoid: Using completely different phrasing or synonyms when original is correct
  • Professional translations: Use verified translations when possible
