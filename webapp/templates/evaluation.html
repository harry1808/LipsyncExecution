‚ÑπÔ∏è About Evaluation

What gets evaluated:

  • WER (Word Error Rate): Measures speech recognition accuracy
  • BLEU Score: Measures translation quality
  • CER (Character Error Rate): Character-level accuracy
  • Lip-Sync: Duration consistency and SyncNet metrics (LSE-D, LSE-C, AV offset) when the output video is available
  • Composite Score: Overall quality rating (0-100)

You will need:

  • Ground truth transcript (what was actually said in the original)
  • Ground truth translation (expected translation in target language)
